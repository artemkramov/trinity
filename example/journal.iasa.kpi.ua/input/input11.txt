Ivchenko</em></div><br/><div id="articleAbstract"><h4>Abstract</h4><br/><div>The qualitative analysis of the Crimea region as a complicated system which includes interdependent units of different nature has been fulfilled. The main purposes and the concept of the competitive sustainable development strategy for the Crimea are developed. The application of the technology forеsight methodology together with the SWOT analysis is proposed for construction of this strategy. By using Porter’s method, the competitive clusters of the Crimea are selected and analyzed in order to continue the complex of works on technological forecast.</div><br/></div><div id="articleFullText"><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://journal.iasa.kpi.ua/article/view/108903/103815" class="file" target="_parent">PDF (Русский)</a></div><div id="GostReferences"> <h4>GOST Style Citations</h4><br/> <div>&mdash;</div></div><br/><br/><div class="separator"></div><h3>Refbacks</h3><ul class="plain"><li>There are currently no refbacks.</li></ul></div></div></div></div></body>
